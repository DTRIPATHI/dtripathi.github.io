Welcome to Yash Global

Yash Global Electronics Security Solution Private Limited Backed by rich industry experience, product knowledge as well as association with leading Security, Networking & Audio/Video products manufacturers, we have been able to successfully gain high recognition in our industry sector. Finding application in residential, industrial and commercial establishments, presently, our expertise lies in offering wide range of Advanced Security Systems, Audio/Video Accessories and Networking Cables.

Established in 2001, Our organization has been engaged in the business of providing comprehensive series of surveillance and security products such as Public Address Systems, Access control systems, Surveillance System, Broadcast Systems, Fire Alarm System, Suppression Systems, Detection Systems, Security System, Home Security System, RFID Solutions Services, Guard Tour Systems Services, Car Park Management Systems, Building Management System, Gas Leak Detection System, Vehicle Tracking Systems and Annual Maintenance Contract Services.
